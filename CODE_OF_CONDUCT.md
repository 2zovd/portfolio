# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on technical merit when reviewing contributions
- Accept feedback gracefully; give it professionally
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication related to this project.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainer directly. Reports will be reviewed and acted upon within a reasonable timeframe. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards, and to ban contributors who repeatedly violate them.
